About ABC Mills

We are passionate about preserving South Indian flavour in every pack of masala powder we create.

Our Story

ABC Mills Pte Ltd began as a family spice mill with a simple goal: deliver pure, authentic masala powders that bring South Indian cooking home.

Our founders grew up around spice farms and traditional stone mills. Today, we carry that craftsmanship forward with modern hygiene and quality standards.

Each batch combines freshly sourced chillies, turmeric, cumin, coriander and more, so every dish reflects the warm depth of South Indian cuisine.
